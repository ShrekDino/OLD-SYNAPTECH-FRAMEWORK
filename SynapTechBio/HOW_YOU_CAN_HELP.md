## The Honest Truth

SynapTechBio is pre-everything. Pre-seed. Pre-revenue. Pre-team. Pre-hardware.

What exists:
- A working IDRE codebase (connectome computation engine)
- A validated FlyWire LSM prototype (>95% accuracy)
- Pitch materials and investor decks
- A clear vision and roadmap
- One founder who is all-in

What doesn't exist (yet):
- Funding
- Hardware (GPU clusters, Loihi chips)
- A team
- Users
- Revenue

**That's where you come in.**

## What Happens When We Get Funded

When investment comes — and it will — here's the commitment:

1. **First priority: the team.** Salaries, benefits, equipment for everyone who helped build.
2. **Second priority: the infrastructure.** GPU clusters, Loihi access, cloud hosting.
3. **Third priority: the community.** Free tier forever, open source continued, governance established.

**Every dollar goes to the people and the mission.** Not yachts. Not corner offices. Not executive bonuses.

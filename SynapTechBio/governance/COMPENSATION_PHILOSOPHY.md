# Compensation Philosophy (Corrected — Deferred)

> *Last updated: May 2026*
> *Status: DESIGN COMPLETE — NOT YET ACTIVE. Deferred until Series A or first paid team member.*

## Current Policy (Pre-Seed)

- **Founder salary:** $0. Minimum draw from savings until Series A.
- **Contributor compensation:** Recognition, co-authorship on publications, and priority equity grants when a priced round closes.
- **No active pay bands. No active equity distribution. No active profit sharing.**

## Deferred Framework (Post-Seed, 3+ FTE)

The following framework is designed and ready for implementation when the team reaches critical mass:

### Principles
- Equal value, equal pay — same role, same pay band
- Transparency — all pay bands published internally
- Living wage floor — no one on the team struggles to meet basic needs
- Equity for everyone — 6+ month contributors receive equity
- Profit sharing — 20% of platform revenue distributed quarterly

### Target Pay Bands (Austin Market — Not Active)

| Role Level | Base Salary | Equity Grant (4yr vest) |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Senior Engineer | $130k - $160k | 1% - 2% |
| Mid Engineer | $100k - $130k | 0.5% - 1% |
| Junior / New Grad | $70k - $90k | 0.25% - 0.5% |
| Research Scientist | $120k - $150k | 1% - 2% |
| Community Manager | $60k - $80k | 0.25% - 0.5% |

### Why Deferred

The original pitch allocated $80k for 2 FTE × 6 months at ~$6.7k/month per engineer — below Austin junior market rate ($70k-$90k annually). This contradicts the "equal value, equal pay" principle. Rather than underpaying early hires, SynapTechBio defers all hires until the sandbox proves traction and STTR grants or revenue justify market-rate compensation.

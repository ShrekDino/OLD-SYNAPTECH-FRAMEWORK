# Ethical Review Board (Deferred)

> *Status: DESIGN COMPLETE — NOT YET ACTIVE. Deferred until the team reaches 6+ FTE.*

## Current Pre-Seed Policy

During the solo-founder phase, ethical decisions are made by the founder with ad-hoc external advisors consulted as needed. No formal board exists.

## Deferred Structure (Post-Seed, 6+ FTE)

The following structure is designed and will be activated when the team reaches critical mass:

- 5-7 members: neuroscientists, ethicists, community representatives, legal advisors
- Staggered terms: 2 years, renewable once
- Binding veto on features with clear ethical concerns
- Public disclosure of all decisions and rationale

**Scope:** New data collection mechanisms, research collaborations with sensitive populations, platform features with dual-use potential, alignment pipeline governance.

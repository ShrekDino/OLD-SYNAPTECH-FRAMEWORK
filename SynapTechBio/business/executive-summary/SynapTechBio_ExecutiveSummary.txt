The Problem
Modern AI is hitting the Silicon Wall. Dense transformers require millions in compute and kW-scale power. Biological
brains run on ~20W. GPT-3 inference uses ~600J per token. The scaling laws are breaking. Standard LLMs cannot
reason about sparse, event-driven biological or neuromorphic systems.

Grounding Reality
Eon Systems recently emulated 125,000 biological neurons to drive a simulated fly body in real-time. This validates that
structural wiring alone can generate complex behavior. SynapTechBio is the compiler that commercializes this insight
for enterprise deployment.

The Solution: IDRE
The Integrated Data Representation Engine (IDRE) is a GPU-accelerated sparse graph engine executing the Drosophila
brain connectome (139k neurons, 50M synapses) in real-time. 5x compute latency reduction and 20x energy savings vs
dense architectures. ~1ms activation on GPU. Real-time 3D brain visualization. Intel Loihi bridge. Data Capture Split
Layer for cryptographic IP protection.

Scientific Validation

~50M synapses, CC BY 4.0. 50+ neuroscientists validated. FlyWire LSM: >95% training accuracy on next-token

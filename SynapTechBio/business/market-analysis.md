# Market Analysis (Corrected)

> *No hockey-stick projections. No TAM/SAM/SOM in pitch decks. Market context only.*

## Context

The global AI infrastructure market is large ($1.8T per Gartner). SynapTechBio does not target a percentage of this market. The go-to-market is a free sandbox targeting a specific niche: connectomics researchers who need a working connectome simulator but cannot access Intel INRC or afford GPU clusters.

## Target Audience (Ordered by Likelihood to Adopt)

| Segment | Size (est.) | Adoption Path |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Drosophila researchers | ~500 labs globally | Direct outreach — they have the dataset, need the tooling |
| Connectomics grad students | ~2,000 | Free sandbox — they'll find it via neurotwitter/GitHub |
| Neuromorphic computing researchers | ~1,000 | Loihi benchmark paper will drive discovery |
| Biotech R&D (Alzheimer's, neural drugs) | ~250 labs | Enterprise inbound — they'll outgrow the sandbox |

## The Founders Opening

**On Slide 1, the words on screen are sparse. But what's said is everything.**

The room expects a polished CEO reading bullet points. What they get is a human being telling them the truth: "I'm one person. I have a working prototype. I need $150k for compute and patents — not a team, not an office, not a management structure."

### Slide 5: The Product — IDRE

**On Screen:** System architecture diagram. Key specs.

**What You Say:**
> *"The Integrated Data Representation Engine. IDRE.*
>
> *It's a GPU-accelerated sparse graph engine that executes the entire Drosophila connectome in real time. On a laptop GPU, activation takes about one millisecond. If the GPU isn't available, it falls back to CPU. There is no single point of hardware failure.*
>
> *Five times latency reduction. Twenty times energy savings. Twenty-five times memory efficiency. On a 1.6 MB memory footprint.*
>
> *It streams neural pulse data to a browser-based 3D visualization. It compiles subgraphs for Intel's Loihi neuromorphic chip. And everything passes through a cryptographic layer that protects researcher IP."*

**Emotional Note:** Technical competence.

**Transition:** *"That cryptographic layer is our moat."* → Click to Slide 6.

### Slide 6: The Moat — DCSL

**On Screen:** DCSL flow diagram — two-path cryptographic fork.

**What You Say:**
> *"The Data Capture Split Layer executes a real-time cryptographic fork on every request.*

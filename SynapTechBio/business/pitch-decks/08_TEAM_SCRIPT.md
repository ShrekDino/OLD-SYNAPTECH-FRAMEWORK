## Delivery Notes

- **Volume:** Start conversational. Get louder on key claims (milestones, metrics). Drop to near-whisper on the close.
- **Eye contact:** Rotate through the room. Never stare at the slides.
- **Hands:** Use gestures for diagrams. Hands still during emotional moments.
- **Pacing:** The close should feel *slow*. Let the weight of the words land.
- **Slide 8 (Sandbox):** If possible, demo the live sandbox rather than show a screenshot. A live demo of a connectome simulation running in the browser is worth more than any slide.
- **Slide 10 (Organization):** Do not apologize for being solo. Own it. It's a feature.
- **Do not:** Read from slides. Memorize the script but deliver conversationally.
- **Do:** Pause. Silence is powerful. Let them lean in.

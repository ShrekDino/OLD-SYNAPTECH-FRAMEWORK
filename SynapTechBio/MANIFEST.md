## The Go-to-Market: Bottom-Up

I am not building a sales team. I am publishing a working connectome simulator to the public web. When a neuroscientist at Stanford hits the rate limit on the free sandbox and emails me asking for more, that is the sales motion — and it costs zero dollars in CAC.

**The three channels, in priority order:**

1. **Free sandbox** — Published URL. Anyone can run 130k neurons in their browser. 100 activations/day free. Opt-in telemetry seeds the data moat.
2. **STTR grants** — Partner with university labs on federal grant applications. Non-dilutive funding that validates the science and builds academic relationships simultaneously.
3. **Enterprise inbound** — When a lab or biotech outgrows the sandbox and requests dedicated infrastructure. Priced per deal, not per tier.

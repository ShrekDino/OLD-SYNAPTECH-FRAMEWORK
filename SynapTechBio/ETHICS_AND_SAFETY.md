# SynapTechBio — Ethics & Safety Framework

> *Open source does not mean unprotected. Building in the open requires building responsibly.*

---

## Our Ethical Foundation

We are building technology that interfaces with the most complex system in the known universe — the brain. This carries profound ethical responsibility.

### Core Principles

1. **Safety is non-negotiable** — No feature, no milestone, no deadline justifies compromising safety
2. **Transparency builds trust** — Every decision, every tradeoff, every limitation is documented openly
3. **Community governs** — Ethical decisions are not made by a single founder, but by the community
4. **IP sovereignty** — Researchers always own their data. We cannot access proprietary information
5. **Dual-use awareness** — We acknowledge that neural technology can be misused and actively build against it

## Safety Protections in the Open Source Model

### What We Open Source

| Component | License | Why Open |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| IDRE platform code | MIT | Community can audit, extend, improve |
| DCSL middleware | MIT | Security through transparency |
| Living Blueprint model weights | Apache 2.0 | Intelligence belongs to the people |
| FlyWire LSM | MIT | Scientific validation, reproducibility |

### What We Protect

| Asset | Protection | Why |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Researcher IP** | AES-256-GCM encrypted before we see it | Without this, no one trusts the platform |
| **DCSL encryption keys** | Never persisted, never shared | Without this, the IP protection is theater |
| **Personal data** | Stripped, hashed, anonymized | Privacy is a human right |
| **Neural data** | Subject to ethical review | Some data should not be collected at all |

### How Open Source Stays Safe

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| **Malicious use of the platform** | DCSL encrypts proprietary IP. We cannot access it, but we can detect abuse patterns in anonymized telemetry |
| **Model misuse** | Living Blueprint weights are open, but we publish ethical use guidelines and reserve the right to revoke platform access |
| **Data poisoning** | Anonymized telemetry is aggregated and anomaly-scored before entering the alignment pipeline |
| **Regulatory non-compliance** | HIPAA/GDPR-ready architecture by design. Ethical review board approval required for sensitive use cases |
| **Runaway alignment** | Continuous alignment pipeline is logged, versioned, and auditable. Model diffs are human-reviewable |

## Dual-Use Policy

We acknowledge that neural technology can be used for both beneficial and harmful purposes.

### What We Build For
- Scientific research and discovery
- Medical diagnostics and treatment
- Educational tools and visualization
- Open scientific collaboration
- Democratized access to neuromorphic computing

### What We Build Against
- Non-consensual neural monitoring
- Cognitive enhancement coercion
- Weaponization of neural interfaces
- Discrimination based on neural data
- Proprietary lock-in of public knowledge

### Enforcement
- Ethical Review Board evaluates potential dual-use concerns
- Platform access can be suspended for violations
- Community whistleblower mechanism (confidential reporting)
- Regular ethical impact assessments

---

## Ethical Review Board (Planned)

The Ethical Review Board is a community-governed body that evaluates ethical implications of platform features, research proposals, and potential dual-use scenarios.

### Structure
- 5-7 members: neuroscientists, ethicists, community representatives, legal advisors
- Staggered terms: 2 years, renewable once
- Diverse perspectives: gender, geography, discipline

### Authority
- Advisory to the Community Council
- Binding veto on features with clear ethical concerns
- Public disclosure of all decisions and rationale

### Scope
- New data collection mechanisms
- Research collaborations with sensitive populations
- Platform features with dual-use potential
- Alignment pipeline governance

## Researcher Safety Commitments

1. **Your data is yours** — Encrypted before we can access it. Exportable at any time.
2. **Your research is yours** — We do not claim ownership of discoveries made on the platform.
3. **Your participation is voluntary** — No lock-in. No data hostage situations.
4. **You will be informed** — Of any changes to privacy, security, or governance.
5. **You have a voice** — Through the community council, RFC process, and ethical review board.

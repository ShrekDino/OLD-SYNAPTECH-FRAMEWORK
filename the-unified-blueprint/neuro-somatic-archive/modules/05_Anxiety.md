# Module 05 — Anxiety Phenotypes

Anxiety phenotypes are interpreted as threat-detection circuit hyperactivation with specific autonomic signatures. Each phenotype maps discrete fear-circuit pathology — amygdalar, brainstem, vestibular, or interoceptive — onto its clinical presentation.

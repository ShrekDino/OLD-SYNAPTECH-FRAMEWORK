# 19. Verbal Rate Analysis

**Physiological Substrate:** Psychomotor speed — dorsolateral PFC/striatal circuitry regulating speech production rate.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Record 60s of spontaneous speech on neutral topic
2. Count: total words, pauses (>2 seconds), filled pauses ("um", "uh")
3. Compute: words per minute, pause frequency

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Measure | Normal | Pathological |
|---|---|---|
| Words per minute | 120–160 | <100 = psychomotor retardation; >180 = pressured speech |
| Pause frequency | 5–15/60s | >20/60s = retrieval poverty; <3/60s = pressured |
| Filled pauses | 3–8/60s | >15/60s = word-finding difficulty / anxiety |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Major Depressive (retardation), Bipolar Mania (pressured), Parkinson's (hypophonia), Schizophrenia (alogia), Social Anxiety (hesitancy), Neurocognitive (reduced fluency).

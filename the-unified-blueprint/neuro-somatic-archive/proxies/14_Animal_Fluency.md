# 14. Animal Fluency (Category Naming)

**Physiological Substrate:** Temporal pole (semantic storage) + dorsolateral PFC (retrieval strategy) — sensitive to frontotemporal and temporal pathology.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Instruct: "Name as many animals as you can in 60 seconds"
2. Count total unique animals (excluding repeats)

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Animals named | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| >18 | Normal |
| 14–18 | Low-average |
| 10–13 | Borderline impairment |
| <10 | Impaired — semantic retrieval deficit |
| <6 | Severe |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Neurocognitive Disorders (Frontotemporal > Alzheimer's), Schizophrenia (negative symptoms), Major Depressive, Autism (restricted categories).

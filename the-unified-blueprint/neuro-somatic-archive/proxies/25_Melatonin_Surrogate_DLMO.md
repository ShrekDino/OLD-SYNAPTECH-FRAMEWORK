# 25. Melatonin Surrogate — Dim Light Melatonin Onset (DLMO) Estimate

**Physiological Substrate:** Pineal melatonin secretion — driven by suprachiasmatic nucleus (SCN) circadian clock; suppressed by light.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Patient sits in dim light (<30 lux) from 18:00 onward
2. Observe for the "sleep gate" — subjective increase in sleepiness
3. Saliva sample at 20:00, 21:00, 22:00 for melatonin (if available)
4. Without assay: use Pittsburgh Sleep Diary to estimate DLMO from sleep onset

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| DLMO Timing | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 20:00–21:00 | Normal |
| After 22:00 | Delayed — phase delay syndrome |
| Before 19:00 | Advanced — phase advance syndrome |
| Absent variation | Non-24-hour or irregular pattern |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Circadian Rhythm Sleep-Wake Disorders (all types), Bipolar Disorder (phase instability), Seasonal Affective, Major Depressive, Delayed Sleep Phase, Shift Work.

# 12. Clock Drawing Test

**Physiological Substrate:** Visuospatial construction, executive planning, frontal-parietal integration.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Provide blank paper
2. Instruct: "Draw a clock face showing all the numbers and set the hands to 10 past 11"
3. Score: 0–3 per domain (visuospatial, numerical, executive)

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Finding | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| Normal | All numbers present, correct spacing, hands correctly placed |
| Mild impairment | Numbers crowded on one side, omitted, or perseverated |
| Moderate | Missing numbers, incorrect hand placement, conceptual deficit |
| Severe | Inability to draw recognizable clock = advanced neurocognitive decline |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Neurocognitive Disorders (Alzheimer's, Frontotemporal, Vascular, Lewy Body), Delirium, Major Depressive (pseudodementia), Schizophrenia (negative symptoms).

# 8. Breath-Hold Challenge

**Physiological Substrate:** Chemoreflex sensitivity — central (medullary) CO₂ detection driving respiratory drive.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Patient seated, exhale fully to functional residual capacity
2. Pinch nose, hold breath
3. Time from end-exhalation to first involuntary breath
4. Record: breath-hold duration in seconds

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Duration | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| >40 s | Normal |
| 25–40 s | Adequate |
| 15–25 s | Reduced chemoreflex tolerance |
| <15 s | Severe — hyperventilation syndrome/serotonergic dysregulation |
| <10 s | Critical — brainstem pathology |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Panic Disorder, Generalized Anxiety, PTSD, Major Depressive Disorder, Anorexia Nervosa (prolonged), Conversion Disorder, Respiratory Panic Subtype, Hyperventilation Syndrome, Bulimia Nervosa.

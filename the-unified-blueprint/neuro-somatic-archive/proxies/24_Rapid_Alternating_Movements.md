# 24. Rapid Alternating Movements (Dysdiadochokinesia)

**Physiological Substrate:** Cerebellar coordination of agonist-antagonist muscle groups.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Patient slaps palm and dorsum of hand alternately on thigh as fast as possible
2. 10 seconds per hand
3. Observe: rhythm, amplitude, speed

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Finding | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| Smooth, rapid, regular | Normal |
| Irregular, slow, uneven amplitude | Cerebellar dysdiadochokinesia |
| Very slow with effort | Basal ganglia/deep cerebellar nuclei |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Cerebellar Degeneration, Neurocognitive Disorders, Multiple Sclerosis, Alcoholism (chronic), Anticonvulsant Toxicity, Huntington's Disease.

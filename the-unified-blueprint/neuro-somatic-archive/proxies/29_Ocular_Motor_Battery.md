# 29. Ocular Motor Battery (Saccade, Pursuit, Optokinetic)

**Physiological Substrate:** Frontal eye fields (saccades), parietal cortex (pursuit), brainstem vestibular nuclei (OKN).

**Administration Protocol:**
1. SACCADE: Hold pen at 30 cm, ask patient to look back and forth between pen and examiner's nose (10 trials)
2. PURSUIT: Slowly move pen in horizontal figure-8, ask patient to follow smoothly
3. OPTOKINETIC: Slowly move striped cloth across visual field, observe nystagmus

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Task | Normal | Pathological |
|---|---|---|
| Saccade latency | <250 ms | >300 ms or catch-up saccades |
| Pursuit gain | Smooth | Saccadic intrusions (cogwheel pursuit) |
| OKN | Symmetric | Asymmetric = parietal lesion |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Schizophrenia (saccadic disinhibition), Parkinson's (hypometric saccades), Progressive Supranuclear Palsy (vertical gaze palsy), Cerebellar (hypermetric saccades), Huntington's (slow saccades), Neurocognitive Disorders.

# 13. Three-Word Recall (Delayed)

**Physiological Substrate:** Hippocampal episodic memory consolidation — entorhinal cortex → dentate gyrus → CA3 pattern completion.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Patient hears 3 unrelated words (e.g., APPLE · TABLE · PENNY)
2. Immediate recall: patient repeats all 3
3. Delay: 3 minutes of intervening conversation
4. Delayed recall: patient recalls all 3

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Words recalled at delay | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 3/3 | Normal |
| 2/3 | Borderline — possible hippocampal strain |
| 1/3 | Abnormal — hippocampal retrieval deficit |
| 0/3 | Severe — likely structural hippocampal pathology |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Neurocognitive Disorders (Alzheimer's most sensitive), Major Depressive (pseudodementia), PTSD (hippocampal suppression), Dissociative Amnesia, Delirium, Schizophrenia.

# 26. Actigraphy — Manual Equivalent (Sleep Log + Behavioral Observation)

**Physiological Substrate:** Rest-activity cycle driven by SCN circadian oscillator.

**Administration Protocol:**
1. Patient completes 7-day sleep log (bedtime, waketime, estimated sleep onset, night wakings)
2. Corroborate with observer (family member) if available
3. Compute: total sleep time, sleep efficiency (time asleep / time in bed × 100), night-to-night variability

**Pathological Thresholds:**
| Measure | Normal | Pathological |
|---|---|---|
| Sleep efficiency | >85% | <80% = insomnia |
| Total sleep time | 7–9h | <6h or >10h = dysregulation |
| Night-to-night variability | <1h | >2h = circadian instability |

**Phenotype Cross-Reference:** Insomnia Disorder, Hypersomnolence Disorder, Circadian Rhythm Disorders, Bipolar Disorder (sleep → episode prediction), Major Depressive, PTSD, Substance Use Disorders.

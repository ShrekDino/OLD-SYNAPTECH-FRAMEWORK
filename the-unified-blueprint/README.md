# The Unified Blueprint

**Integrating Social Liberation into the Socialist Foundation**

*The People's Institute for Intersectional Materialist Studies — May 2026*

---

## Synopsis

This book argues that the exclusion of LGBTQIA+ and racial minorities from the core theoretical and constitutional architecture of socialism is not a minor oversight — it is a structural vulnerability that has been systematically exploited by capitalist powers to fracture leftist movements, alienate potential allies, and provide ideological ammunition for reactionary forces.

It offers a blueprint for a civilization in which economic planning and social liberation are not competing priorities but unified pillars of a single revolutionary project. Through the framework of **Intersectional Materialism**, it synthesizes class analysis with the concrete realities of race, gender, and sexuality, developing both the theoretical foundations and the constitutional architecture for a socialism adequate to the twenty-first century.

## The Evolutionary Lens

Before reading the manuscript, consider the framing offered in the **[Foreword: The Great Handoff](foreword.md)** — an essay that reinterprets capitalism, socialism, and communism not as competing ideologies but as **evolutionary stages of a species' technological metabolism**:

> Capitalism is the metabolic necessity — a high-energy, high-waste growth phase.
> Socialism is the nervous system — the coordination phase that distributes gains to the edges.
> Communism is the technological event horizon — possible only once automated agents handle the thermodynamic cost of survival.

*The Unified Blueprint* is a design document for the coordination phase. Read it through this lens.

The Unified Blueprint extends beyond political economy into clinical and somatic domains. The companion **[Neuro-Somatic Archive](neuro-somatic-archive/)** is a complete forensic reclassification of the DSM-5 — 220+ phenotypes reinterpreted through the lens of allostatic load, entropy thresholds, and zero-cost biological proxy protocols.
